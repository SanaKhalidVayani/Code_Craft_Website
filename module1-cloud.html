Module 1: Cloud Computing Basics

Introduction to cloud computing, its benefits, and popular cloud services.

Lesson Video

Notes & Transcript

  • Cloud computing delivers services over the internet
  • Services include storage, servers, and databases
  • No need for physical hardware
  • Scalable and cost-effective
  • Examples: Google Drive, AWS, Azure
