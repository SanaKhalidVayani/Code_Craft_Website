Module 1: Introduction to Artificial Intelligence

Learn the basics of AI, how it works, and real-life applications.

Lesson Video

Notes & Transcript

  • Artificial Intelligence (AI) is the ability of machines to mimic human intelligence
  • AI systems can learn, reason, and make decisions
  • Examples: voice assistants, recommendation systems, chatbots
  • AI works using data and algorithms
  • AI is used in healthcare, finance, education, and more
